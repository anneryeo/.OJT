WHY THIS MATTERS

A portfolio bridge between Mapua formation and industry opportunity.

This prototype supports Mapua's technology-driven identity by turning student output into searchable evidence: projects, skills, course paths, year levels, availability, and measurable outcomes.

For a university known for engineering, computing, innovation, and industry relevance, a registry like this helps students present work professionally while giving partners a faster way to discover qualified talent.
